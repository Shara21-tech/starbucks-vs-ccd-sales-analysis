*** Starbucks vs CCD Sales Analysis & Business Intelligence Dashboard

** Project Overview

This project analyzes and compares the sales performance of Starbucks and Café Coffee Day (CCD) using SQL, Excel, and Power BI.

The objective is to identify customer trends, product performance, store-level performance, and revenue patterns while providing data-driven recommendations that can help improve business performance and increase sales.

** Business Problem

In the highly competitive coffee retail industry, understanding customer behavior and sales performance is essential for growth.

This project aims to answer key business questions:

Which products generate the highest revenue?
Which stores perform the best?
Which customer segments contribute most to sales?
How do Starbucks and CCD compare in terms of sales performance?
What strategies can Starbucks implement to increase revenue and customer engagement?

** Dashboard Insights

* Product Performance
-A small number of products contribute a significant share of total revenue.
-Coffee-based products consistently outperform other categories.
-Top-performing products should receive additional marketing support and promotional visibility.

* Category Analysis
-Coffee categories generate the highest revenue contribution.
-Some non-core categories contribute relatively lower revenue and may require product optimization or targeted promotions.

* Store Performance
-Revenue varies significantly across store locations.
-High-performing stores can be used as benchmarks for operational best practices.

* Customer Analysis
-Customer concentration is highest in major urban areas.
-Customer demand patterns vary across locations, creating opportunities for localized marketing campaigns.

* Revenue Trends
-Revenue remains relatively stable across periods with noticeable peak sales intervals.
-Seasonal and promotional opportunities can be leveraged to maximize revenue growth.


** Starbucks vs CCD Comparison

* Starbucks Strengths
-Strong premium brand image.
-Higher customer spending potential.
-Strong performance in premium beverage categories.
-Consistent revenue contribution from flagship products.

* CCD Strengths
-Wider accessibility for price-sensitive customers.
-Competitive pricing strategy.
-Strong appeal among students and budget-conscious consumers.

* Comparative Findings
-Starbucks performs strongly in premium product segments.
-CCD benefits from affordability and broader accessibility.
-Customer purchasing behavior suggests opportunities for Starbucks to expand offerings targeted at value-conscious customers while maintaining premium positioning.

** Recommendations to Increase Starbucks Sales
1. Expand Value-Based Combos

Introduce beverage + snack combo offers during non-peak hours to increase average order value.

2. Focus on High-Performing Products

Promote top-selling products through targeted campaigns and seasonal offers.

3. Strengthen Customer Loyalty Programs

Enhance rewards and membership benefits to increase repeat purchases.

4. Improve Underperforming Store Performance

Analyze operational practices of top-performing stores and replicate successful strategies.

5. Location-Based Marketing

Use city-level customer insights to create localized promotions and targeted advertising campaigns.

6. Cross-Selling Opportunities

Recommend complementary food items alongside popular beverages to increase basket size.

7. Seasonal Promotions

Leverage festivals, holidays, and seasonal trends to drive additional customer traffic.
